Tic Tac Toe

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Two players take turns, one using "" and the other using ""
  • The objective is to get three of your marks in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • Players take turns placing their mark in an empty cell of the grid.
  • The game ends when one player gets three of their marks in a row, or when all cells are filled, resulting in a draw.
  • If a player wins, they are declared the winner. If all cells are filled with no winner, the game is a draw.
